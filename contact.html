Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

I recommend booking at least 2 to 4 weeks in advance for photography shoots, and 1 to 2 weeks for content creation projects. For larger campaigns, earlier is always better.

Do you travel for shoots?

Absolutely! I am based in Accra, Ghana but available for travel nationally and internationally. Travel costs will be discussed and agreed upon before booking.

How long until I receive my photos?

Edited photos are typically delivered within 5 to 7 business days after the shoot. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

How can I appear on your podcast?

Fill in the contact form above and select Podcast Guest Appearance as the subject. Tell me a little about yourself and what value you would bring to my audience.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! For larger projects I offer flexible payment plans. A deposit is required to secure your booking and the remainder is due upon delivery.
